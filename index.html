Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yes, it's completely free! We partner with brands who want to get their gift cards into the hands of potential customers. The only requirement is completing a quick email verification to prevent abuse of the system.

Why do I need to verify my email?

Email verification helps us ensure that each person only claims one gift card, making the program fair for everyone. It also helps prevent fraud and automated claims.

How quickly will I receive my gift card?

Most users receive their gift card code instantly after completing verification. In rare cases, it may take up to 24 hours if additional verification is needed.

Can I claim more than one gift card?

Currently, we limit one gift card per person to ensure as many people as possible can benefit from this program.

Is my personal information safe?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell your information to third parties. Your email is only used for verification and to send your gift card.
